About

I don't just manage products.
I ship them.

My career started in fiber engineering at AT&T, where I learned that good planning is the difference between a project that launches and one that sits in review. That foundation shaped everything that came after.

Over the next several years I moved into product ownership and program management, taking on initiatives where the path forward wasn't always clear. I got comfortable with ambiguity, learned to align people who had different incentives, and built a habit of asking what "done" actually means before anyone writes a line of code.

Most recently I led WiFi 6 and WiFi 7 hardware strategy from concept through market launch, which meant sitting at the intersection of engineering, vendor management, marketing, and the customer experience all at once. That's the kind of work I enjoy.
